How This Came to Be

Our State invited people of faith from the coast to the mountains — rural preachers, urban ministers, and religious scholars — to share their interpretation of “a prayer for North Carolina.” The result: an inspirational celebration of the state's geography, culture, history, spirit, and its people. Here you will find a first-of-its-kind approach to storytelling from Our State.

We invite you to listen to the essays as read by their writers and to explore the beauty of North Carolina that inspired these prayers. Enjoy exclusive video and musical selections as you experience each prayer in depth.
